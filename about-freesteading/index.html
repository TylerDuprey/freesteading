About Freesteading.com

Freesteading.com is intended to be your all inclusive resource for finding, joining, or visiting free startup societies and communities all over the world and eventually the solar system.

We are dedicated to the principles of freedom and voluntaryism. Every peaceful person should be free from coercion and free to chose their community structures.
